Jin tonic 만드는 법

Jin tonic is one of tranditional long drink cocktail in the world. it can be made very simple and easy - just with Jin, Tonic, Lime or Lemon

How to make Jin tonic
1. Prepare Jin 40ml, tonic 120ml, 2 slides of lime
2. Put jin and tonic into glass with ice
3. Squeze lime into glass
4. Stir all ingredients
