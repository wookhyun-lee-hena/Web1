Long Island iced tea 만드는 법

Long Island iced tea is a type of alcoholic mixed drink typically made with vodka, tequila, light rum, triple sec, gin, and a splash of cola, which gives the drink the same amber hue as its namesake.

How to make Long Island iced tea
1. Prepare Jin 1/2 oz, Vodka 1/2 oz, white rum 1/2 oz, Tequila 1/2 oz, Triple Sec 1/2 oz, Sweet and Sour mix 11/2 oz, Coke, Lemon slice
2. Put crushed ice into glass
3. Stir with all ingredients in glass
4. Put lemon slice
